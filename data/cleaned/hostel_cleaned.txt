## Sports & Recreation
JECRC University emphasizes the "Five Ss" of sports training: Stamina, Speed, Strength, Skill, and Spirit.

Facilities: A massive sports area spanning 35,000 sqm.

Offered Sports: * Outdoor: Football, Basketball, Volleyball, American Football (Tackle & Flag), Cricket, and Kabaddi.

Indoor: Table Tennis, Badminton, and Chess.

Coaching: Professional guidance and experienced coaches are available to prepare students for intercollegiate, state, and national competitions.

Flagship Event: “JU VERVE”, an annual national sports event to discover and showcase athletic talent.

Recent Achievements (2023-24):

Gold Medals: Basketball (Girls) at Athlon & Vanquish; Football at Arya Cup; Volleyball (Boys) at Vanquish.

National Level: Bronze in Valorant (Men) at the All India University (AIU) Championship.

## Health & Wellness
The university maintains a holistic support system for physical and mental well-being.

Gymnasium: A well-equipped, modern workout space available for students to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Medical Services: * 24/7 Assistance: On-campus medical facility for minor illnesses and emergency care.

Ambulance: 24-hour ambulance service stationed at the hostel premises.

Preventative Care: Regular health screenings and vaccination drives.

Mental Health: Free counseling sessions provided through a collaboration with the Aditya Birla Education Trust.

## Campus Dining: The Food Court
Space: A newly designed, 18,000 sq. ft. fully air-conditioned cafeteria.

Brands: Features popular outlets such as Domino’s, Burger Singh, Moti Mahal, Chai Garam, and Divine Dosa.

Experience: Offers a sleek, modern environment that redefines standard campus dining.

## Residential Life (Hostels)
JECRC University offers a "home away from home" experience through its partnership with Stanza Living.

Living Arrangements
Unit Design: Apartment-style units where 3–4 private, single-occupancy rooms open into a common living area.

Amenities: Rooms include wardrobes, study desks, and Wi-Fi. Common areas feature a television (optional in AC rooms).

Climate Control: The campus uses a unique cavity wall exposed brick concept, creating an 8°C to 10°C temperature difference between indoors and outdoors naturally.

The Stanza Living Experience
Housekeeping: Professional staff for regular cleaning.

Dining: Tailor-made menus suited to regional palettes, managed via a tech-driven app.

Security: 24/7 support, thermal monitoring at entry/exit points, and "COVID-19 Combat Ready" hygiene protocols.

Key Hostel Rules
Attendance: Compulsory daily attendance at 10:00 PM.

Gates Closing: 9:00 PM for Girls; 10:00 PM for Boys.

Out Pass:

Boys: 5:00 PM – 8:30 PM (Daily).

Girls: 5:00 PM – 6:00 PM (Daily); 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Sundays/Holidays).

General Conduct: Smoking, gambling, and alcohol are strictly prohibited. Celebrating birthdays inside rooms is not allowed.

## Eco-Friendly Campus Design
The 32-acre campus is designed as a Green Campus:

Energy Efficiency: Uses natural light, cross-ventilation, and local materials like Kota and Jodhpur stone.

Sustainability: Zero-waste campus featuring water recycling for irrigation and extensive rainwater harvesting.

Biodiversity: Home to over 90 different species of plantation.
