## Admission Dates and Process (2026)

Admissions for 2026 are open now. Apply online at jecrcuniversity.edu.in.
Application deadline: typically August 31, 2026 (may extend per UGC guidelines).
Admission cycle opens post-board results (March/April) and closes late August.
No entrance exam conducted by JECRC. JEE Main scores preferred but not mandatory.
Direct admission possible based on Class 12th merit (60% aggregate in PCM) if JEE score is low.
Management quota available for seats beyond merit and JEE criteria.

## Documents Required for Admission

10th marksheet and certificate.
12th marksheet and certificate.
JEE Main scorecard (if available).
Transfer certificate.
Character certificate.
Aadhaar card or valid ID proof.
4 to 6 passport size photographs.
Category certificate (if applicable for SC/ST/OBC/Women).
Migration certificate.
Originals plus photocopies required at the time of document verification.

## Lateral Entry (Direct 2nd Year Admission)

Eligible with AICTE-approved diploma (minimum 60% in relevant branch).
Also eligible with B.Sc. degree (minimum 60%) with Mathematics in 10+2.
5% relaxation for SC, ST, OBC, and Women candidates.
Available for branches including CSE and ECE.

## NRI and Foreign Student Admissions

Separate process via NRI quota.
Apply online with equivalent eligibility documents, passport, and visa proofs.
Contact admissions office for specific requirements: admission@jecrcu.edu.in.

## Hostel Application at Admission

Apply for hostel simultaneously during main admission process.
Requires same documents as admission plus a hostel-specific form.
No guarantee of hostel seat. Allotted on first-come first-served basis.

## B.Tech Admission Eligibility

Minimum 60% aggregate marks in 10+2 (Senior Secondary).
Mandatory subjects: English, Physics, and Mathematics.
Optional subject (one of): Chemistry, Biology, Biotechnology, or Technical Vocational subject.
JEE (Main) scores are prioritized for selection.
Category relaxation: 5% relaxation (55% minimum) for SC, ST, Rajasthan OBC (Non-creamy layer), and Women candidates.

Annual tuition fee: Rs. 2,75,000.

GST charged extra as applicable.

## Scholarship Schemes (Based on 10+2 Marks)

Scholarships apply to Tuition Fee only. Only one scholarship awarded at a time (highest applicable).

70% to 84.99% aggregate in 10+2: 15% tuition fee waiver. Applicable to all UG programs.
85% and above aggregate in 10+2: 25% tuition fee waiver. Applicable to all UG programs.
JEE (Main) 2026 Percentile 75 to 89.99 (General Category): 15% tuition fee waiver. Applicable to all B.Tech programs.
JEE (Main) 2026 Percentile above 90 (General Category): 25% tuition fee waiver. Applicable to all B.Tech programs.
CAT 2025 Percentile 40 to 59.99: 15% tuition fee waiver. Applicable to MBA programs.
CAT 2025 Percentile above 60: 25% tuition fee waiver. Applicable to MBA programs.

## Additional Scholarship (10% one-time)

Available for: NCC A Certificate holders, Alumni children, Siblings of current students, JU Employee children, Single Mother, Single Girl Child, War Widow, Father from Ex-Services or Para-Military killed in action, Sports Medalist at minimum State level tournament recognized by Indian Olympic Association.
Only one additional scholarship given at a time.

## Scholarship Continuation Requirements

Minimum 8.0 CGPA (SGPA in 1st semester) every year.
Minimum 75% attendance in every individual subject (Theory, Lab, Project, Seminar).
No compartments (failed subjects) in any semester.
No disciplinary actions or punishments.
Scholarship reviewed annually at end of even semester.

## Scholarship Restoration Policy

If withdrawn due to low CGPA or attendance: may be restored after next review if criteria met.
If withdrawn due to indiscipline: never restored under any circumstances.
CGPA of 7.99 or below results in immediate forfeiture. No rounding off.
Attendance of exactly 75% or below also results in forfeiture.

## Scholarship Restrictions

Students receiving financial assistance from Government or private organizations are not eligible.
University reserves the right to discontinue scholarship schemes at its discretion.
Final scholarship awarding decision rests with appropriate university bodies.

## Chairperson Fellowship

Available for students from EWS, OBC-NC, SC, ST categories.
Family income must be below Rs. 3 lakhs per year.
Scholarship amount: 15% of tuition fee.
Maximum 50 fellowships awarded per year.

Students without JEE scores can also apply directly based on 10+2 merit.
