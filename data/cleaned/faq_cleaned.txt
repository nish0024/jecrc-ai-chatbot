## About JECRC University
JECRC University is a private university established in 2012 under the Rajasthan Private University Act.
It is recognized by UGC and approved by AICTE.
NAAC accredited with B++ grade (CGPA 2.65).
NBA accredited programs: B.Tech CSE, ECE, IT, and ME.
Medium of instruction is English for all programs.

## Admission Process
B.Tech eligibility: 10+2 with minimum 60% marks in PCM.
5% relaxation for SC, ST, OBC, and women candidates.
PG eligibility: Relevant graduation degree.
Accepted entrance exams for B.Tech: JEE Main, CUET-UG, REAP.
Other programs: Admission on merit or direct basis.
Application form is available online with a fee of ₹850.
Process steps: Online application, merit list or counselling, document verification.
Lateral entry available for B.Tech: Diploma holders with minimum 60% marks.

## Hostel & Residential Facilities
JECRC offers apartment-style hostel accommodation for both boys and girls.
Each unit has individual private rooms connected by a shared common living area.
Hostel allotment is on availability basis and is not guaranteed.
Hostels are generally allotted for the first two years (four semesters) for UG students.
Free Wi-Fi is included in all hostel units.
Gymnasium access is optional at ₹1,000 per semester.
One-time non-refundable registration fee: ₹5,000.
