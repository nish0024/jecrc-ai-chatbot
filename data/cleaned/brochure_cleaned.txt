## JECRC University Overview (Academic Session 2025-26)

JECRC University (established in 2012) is an institution focused on outcome-based education and research. The campus is located in Jaipur, Rajasthan, and currently hosts over 21,000 active students and an alumni network of 30,000+.

---

### Admission & Merit Preparation
* **Application Fee:** ₹850 (All programs) | ₹1,500 (Ph.D. program).
* **B.Tech Selection:** 1.  JEE-Main 2025 merit.
    2.  CUET-UG 2025 merit.
    3.  Aggregate percentage in 10+2 (Min. 60% aggregate).
* **Special Requirements:** * **B.Des/BVA:** Portfolio assessment and personal interview.
    * **Law/BA-JMC/MBA:** Personal interview/GD qualifying.
    * **Laptops:** Mandatory for B.Tech, B.Des, BVA, BCA, MCA, MBA, M.Des, and MVA students.
