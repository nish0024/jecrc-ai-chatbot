## Internship Program
Six-month internship is mandatory for all final-year students across all disciplines.
Total internship offers: 11,470+.
61% of internships are paid with monthly stipends ranging from ₹5,000 to ₹60,000.
Internship duration: 4 to 6 months, integrated into the curriculum.
Internships frequently convert into full-time jobs through Pre-Placement Offers (PPOs).
102 students recently secured internships at ISRO and DRDO.
JECRC ranked 5th nationwide by Internshala for internship quality.
Students apply for internships through the university portal or company drives coordinated by the placement cell.
A dedicated Directorate of Internship manages the program with faculty guides mentoring students.

## Training & Placement Cell
The Training and Placement Cell runs a Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) program before placement drives.
CRT integrated program: 500+ hours.
CRT intensive program: 200 hours.
Training covers: Aptitude (quantitative, logical, verbal), technical coding, mock interviews, resume building, group discussions, and psychometric evaluations.
Specialized tech training includes: Python, Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Salesforce, AI, and Robotic Process Automation.
The cell also supports students with off-campus applications through resume building and training.

## Alumni Network
A formal alumni network is available for current students.
Alumni provide referrals and mentorship to support placements and career growth.

## New School Placement Cell
A specialized placement unit exists for students in Design, Hospitality, Journalism, Humanities, and Law.
Focuses on internship-cum-placement ecosystems suited to non-engineering disciplines.

## JU Makerspace
North India's first student-driven innovation community.
Equipment includes large-scale 3D printers (1 cubic meter capacity) and digital oscilloscopes.
Students build prototypes and develop an entrepreneurial mindset valued by recruiters.
