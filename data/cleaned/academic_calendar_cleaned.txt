## JECRC University Academic Calendar (Session 2025-2026)

Note: This calendar applies to all schools except the School of Law (though first-year dates are common).

## Semester Dates

Odd Semester (1st Year): Begins August 05, 2025. Classes start August 11, 2025.
Odd Semester (Upper Years): Begins August 18, 2025. Classes start August 18, 2025.
Even Semester (All Years): Begins January 19, 2026. Classes start January 19, 2026.

2nd Mid-Term: November 08-14, 2025 (Odd Sem). April 20-25, 2026 (Even Sem).
3rd Mid-Term: December 04-06, 2025 (Odd Sem). May 21-23, 2026 (Even Sem).

Practical Exams (Even Sem): May 25-28, 2026.

End-Term Exams (Even Sem): June 01-15, 2026.

Semester Ends (Even): June 15, 2026.

## Supplementary Exams

Odd Sem Supplementary: January 02-13, 2026.
Even Sem Supplementary: June 16-28, 2026.

## Key Events

Orientation for 1st Year Students: August 05-08, 2025.
Annual Fest (Rhythm): March 05-07, 2026.

Next Odd Session Starts: August 03, 2026.

## Results and Portal

Results are declared on the student portal at sws.jecrcuniversity.edu.in using roll number and date of birth.
Backlog and supplementary results follow main exam result declarations.
Fee payment deadlines are notified via fee demand notices on the university website and student portal.
Internship period typically aligns with summer break (June-July).
