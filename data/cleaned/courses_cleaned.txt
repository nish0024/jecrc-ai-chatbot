
JECRC University Jaipur offers clearly defined program durations, seat intakes, and rankings that address common student queries during admissions. It's a private self-financed university (established 2012 under Rajasthan Govt Act), not affiliated with Rajasthan University, with English as the primary medium of instruction across all programs. [collegedunia](https://collegedunia.com/news/jecrc-jaipur-fee-structure-2026-released-alertid-149631)

## Program Durations
Standard durations match industry norms:

- B.Tech: 4 years
- BCA: 3 years (Pass) or 4 years (Honours)
- BBA/B.Com/B.Sc/BA/B.Des: 3-4 years
- MBA/MCA/M.Tech: 2 years
- Integrated LLB (BA/BBA/B.Sc LLB): 5 years
- M.Sc/MA/LLM/M.Des: 2 years [collegedunia](https://collegedunia.com/news/jecrc-jaipur-fee-structure-2026-released-alertid-149631)
