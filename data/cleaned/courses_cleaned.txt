Academic Schools & Degree Programs
1. School of Engineering & Technology
UG (B.Tech): Core branches in Civil, EE, ECE (Semiconductor/Chip Design), and Mechanical (including Electric Vehicles).

B.Tech CSE Specializations: * Industry-Partnered: Gaming Tech, Fintech & AI (PayTM), Software Product Engineering (Kalvium), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (Xebia/Samatrix/IBM), Cyber Security (EC-Council), and Cloud Computing (Microsoft/AWS).

Strategic Aligned: Computer Science and Business Systems (TCS) and Generative AI (L&T EduTech).

PG: M.Tech in CSE, Civil, EE, ECE, and Mechanical.

2. School of Computer Applications
UG (BCA): 3-Year Pass / 4-Year Honours degrees.

Specializations: Health Informatics, AI & Data Science, Cloud Automation, Cyber Security, and Full Stack Development (IBM/Xebia).

PG (MCA): 2-Year programs in General MCA, AI & Data Science, Cyber Security, and Cloud Computing.

3. Jaipur School of Business
UG: BBA (various core specializations) and B.Com (including Capital Markets and International Accreditations).

Specialized BBA: Fintech (Deloitte/Zell), Global Business (ISDC), and Data Analytics.

PG (MBA): Dual Specialization in core HR/Marketing/Finance.

Specialized MBA: Applied Finance (Deloitte), AI, Data Analytics, Fintech (Imarticus), and Hospital & Healthcare Management.

4. School of Design & Mass Communication
Jaipur School of Design: * UG: B.Des in Interior, Fashion, Jewellery, Game Art & Animation, and Communication Design.

PG: M.Des and M.Sc. in Fashion, Interior, Graphic, and Jewellery Design.

Jaipur School of Mass Communication:

UG: B.A. Journalism & Mass Communication (3/4 Years).

PG: M.A. in Journalism & Mass Communication and Filmmaking.

5. School of Law
UG (Integrated): B.A. LL.B (Hons.), B.Sc. LL.B (Hons.), and BBA LL.B (Hons.).

PG (LL.M): 2-Year specializations in Business Law, IPR, and Personal Law.

6. School of Humanities, Economics & Social Sciences
Humanities: B.A. (Hons.) in English, Political Science, and Psychology.

Liberal Studies: Majors in International Relations, Public Policy, Linguistics, and Family Business.

Economics: B.A. (Hons.) and M.A. in Economics.

PG: M.A. in Public Policy, Psychology (Clinical), and International Relations.

7. School of Sciences & Allied Health
Sciences: B.Sc. (Hons.) and M.Sc. in Forensic Science, Biotechnology, and Microbiology. Core M.Sc. in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, and Zoology.

Allied Health: M.Sc. in Clinical Embryology (Indira IVF) and MPT (Sports, Ortho, Neuro, Cardio).

Hospitality: B.Sc. in Hospitality and Hotel Management (HHM).
