## Scholarship Tiers (2026-2027)
Scholarships are calculated as a percentage of the tuition fee only, not total fees.

Merit-Based (10+2 Aggregate):
70% to 84.99% aggregate: 15% scholarship on tuition fee. Eligible for B.Tech and all UG programs.
85% and above aggregate: 25% scholarship on tuition fee. Eligible for B.Tech and all UG programs.

JEE Main (B.Tech Only):
75 to 89.99 percentile: 15% scholarship on tuition fee.
90 percentile and above: 25% scholarship on tuition fee.

CAT 2025 (MBA Only):
40 to 59.99 percentile: 15% scholarship on tuition fee.
60 percentile and above: 25% scholarship on tuition fee.

Additional One-Time Scholarships (First Year Only):
10% scholarship for: NCC A certificate holders, alumni children, siblings of current students, single mother candidates, state-level sports medalists.
Additional scholarships apply to all programs.
Additional scholarships cannot be stacked with merit-based scholarships. Verify current stacking rules with the admissions office.

## Scholarship Maintenance Rules
Minimum 8.0 CGPA must be maintained every semester to continue the scholarship.
No backlogs or compartments are allowed in any semester.
Minimum 75% attendance required in all courses including theory, lab, and projects.
Any act of indiscipline or written warning results in permanent withdrawal of the scholarship with no restoration.
If scholarship is lost due to academics or attendance, it can be restored after the next even semester review if the student meets 8.0 CGPA and 75% attendance at that review.

## Fee Payment Schedule
1st installment: Due at the time of admission or by 30th April.
2nd installment: Due by 10th to 15th November each year.

## Late Fee Policy
Late payment fine: ₹500 per day for up to 10 days after the due date.
If fees remain unpaid beyond 10 days, the student is struck off the rolls.

## Refund Policy
Refund follows UGC norms.
A processing deduction of ₹5,000 applies on all refund requests.
A prorated deduction of 1/12th of annual tuition per month also applies if withdrawing before classes begin.
No refund is issued after classes have started.

## Eligibility Relaxation
5% relaxation in minimum eligibility marks for SC, ST, Rajasthan State OBC non-creamy layer, and women category candidates.
