## Campus Policies

Dress Code: No strict uniform. Neat and formal attire expected during classes and events.

Library Timings: 9 AM to 9 PM daily (12 hours). Extended access available for researchers.

Wi-Fi: Free campus-wide Wi-Fi for all students. Login via student portal. Fair usage limits apply.

Campus Timings: Opens 8 AM for day scholars. Gates close at 6 PM.
Hostellers have separate access beyond 6 PM.
